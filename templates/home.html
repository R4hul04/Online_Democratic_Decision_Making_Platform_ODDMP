Online Voting


  • In "ONLINE VOTING SYSTEM" a voter can use his/her voting right online without any difficulty. He/She has to be registered first for him/her to vote and enjoy services provide by the system such as voting. If invalid/wrong details are submitted, then the citizen is not registered to vote.
